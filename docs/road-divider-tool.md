## Overview

Road Divider Tool divides a road into two connected roads

## Divide Road
1. Click the `Road Divider Tool` button.
2. Click the road you want to chop. 
3. A line following the pointer appears, indicating where the road will be divided
4. Move the pointer so that line is at the desired location. Then, press `SHIFT` and `LEFT CLICK` to chop the road.

## Undo
1. You can undo by pressing `CTRL + Z` or from Menu `Edit->Undo`
2. This will remove the last created division
